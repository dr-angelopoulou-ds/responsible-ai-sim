Student guidance statement

You may use AI tools to support learning (brainstorming, outlining, explanations). Submitted work must reflect your own understanding. Verify key facts with course materials or reliable sources, and follow the course policy on disclosure/citation. Do not enter private or sensitive information into AI tools.

Faculty transparency statement

AI tools may be used to support learning and feedback. When AI assistance is used for instructional materials or feedback, it is reviewed by a human instructor. Students will be informed when and how AI is used, and accessibility, fairness, and privacy considerations guide all AI use.

Responsible AI checklist

Before using AI:
• Who is accountable for the final output/decision?
• How will outputs be verified?
• How will AI use be disclosed?
• What accessibility needs must be met?
• What privacy risks exist?
• Are the stakes high? If yes, increase safeguards.
